Xschiq

AI-Driven Precision Psychiatry

Our Mission

Xschiq is pioneering the next generation of mental health tools by leveraging artificial intelligence to personalize psychiatric treatment. Our platform predicts how individuals will respond to both FDA-approved and experimental antipsychotics, including TAAR1 and M4 modulators.

What We Do

  • Predict response to D2 partial agonists and investigational drugs
  • Integrate multimodal data: EEG, genomics, fMRI, and digital behavior
  • Support personalized treatment and clinical trial optimization
  • Deliver a scalable SaaS solution for psychiatry clinics and biotech partners

Market Opportunity

The AI in mental health market is projected to grow from $1.3B in 2024 to over $14B by 2033. Precision psychiatry alone is expected to reach $4.3B in the same period. Xschiq is positioned at the intersection of these fast-growing domains.
